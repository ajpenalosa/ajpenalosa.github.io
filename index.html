About Me

My name is AJ Penalosa and I am a Web Designer living in the beautiful city of Los Angeles, CA. I have been designing, developing and maintaining websites since 2005. All websites are custom built with the user in mind to ensure that visitors have a positive and lasting experience.

I completed a three year program at Westwood College of Technology. I graduated in July of 2006 and received a Bachelor of Science Degree in Animation. The Animation program at Westwood College covered all aspects of design. I was exposed not only to 3D modeling and animation, but also to web design and print media. This broad range of experience helped me obtain a job as an in-house web designer for a local camera store. I worked there for three years and quickly grew a passion for web design.

In 2007, I decided I needed a change of pace. Having lived in Los Angeles my whole life, I wanted to experience life “away from home”. I was fresh out of college with a Bachelors Degree, so I figured it was the perfect opportunity. Las Vegas was a booming city, so I saw it as the best place to start off. I landed a job working full-time as a web designer for one of the leading companies in automotive online marketing.

Fast forward to 2017 and I find myself back in Los Angeles, focusing 100% on Pixel Motives. The software I use are Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Animate and Coda 2 by Panic Inc.

If you like what you’ve seen or have any comments, please don’t hesitate to contact me.
